ABOUT

This interactive online experience was created to document one simple concept: change over time. Through the use of a scrollable map and icons and their corresponding images, the viewer is able to experience the deterioration that occurs to the soles of shoes as their owner wears them. Through this, I hope to suggest the idea that change is inevitable, and that embracing it will make your journies, however you may define them, that much more rewarding.

Wear your shoes proudly. Let their soles fade down to nothingness and feel the dirt below you.
